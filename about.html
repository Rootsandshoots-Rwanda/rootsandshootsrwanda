What Is Roots & Shoots?

Roots & Shoots is a global youth-led community action program founded by Dr. Jane Goodall. It empowers young people to become compassionate leaders and create positive change in their communities.

People

Improving lives and fostering understanding in our communities

Animals

Protecting wildlife and promoting animal welfare

Environment

Conserving and restoring our shared natural world

Compassion → Action → Change

Our philosophy is simple yet powerful: compassion inspires action, and collective action creates meaningful change in our world.

Our Mission, Vision & Goal

Guiding principles that drive our work in Rwanda

Mission

To empower Rwandan youth with knowledge, tools, and inspiration to become compassionate leaders creating sustainable change for people, animals, and the environment.

Vision

A Rwanda where young people are actively engaged in building sustainable communities that live in harmony with nature and each other.

Goal

To establish Roots & Shoots clubs in every district of Rwanda, engaging 50,000 young people in community action projects by 2030.

About Dr. Jane Goodall

Dr. Jane Goodall, DBE, founder of the Jane Goodall Institute and UN Messenger of Peace, is a world-renowned ethologist and conservationist inspiring greater understanding and protection of all life on Earth.

In 1991, Dr. Goodall founded Roots & Shoots with a group of Tanzanian students. The program began with the goal of bringing together youth from preschool to university age to work on environmental, conservation, and humanitarian issues.

"Young people, when informed and empowered, when they realize that what they do truly makes a difference, can change the world."

- Dr. Jane Goodall

Roots & Shoots Rwanda Story

How Roots & Shoots Came to Rwanda

Roots & Shoots was introduced to Rwanda in 2008 by a group of inspired educators and environmentalists who believed in Dr. Goodall's vision of youth-led change.

Starting with just three schools in Kigali, the movement quickly spread across the country as young Rwandans embraced the opportunity to address local challenges through community action projects.

  • Officially registered with the Rwanda Governance Board in 2010
  • Recognized by the Ministry of Education for youth empowerment
  • Partnered with 200+ schools and communities nationwide

Our Founding Story

Roots & Shoots Rwanda was founded by a coalition of educators, conservationists, and youth leaders who witnessed the transformative power of Dr. Goodall's approach during visits to neighboring Tanzania.

From the beginning, our organization has been volunteer-driven, with passionate individuals dedicating their time to mentor youth and facilitate community projects.
